## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AI Content Transparency Disclaimer on Home Page
The home landing page ("Discover") SHALL display an AI content transparency disclaimer to inform users immediately upon visiting the platform.

#### Scenario: User visits the home page
- **WHEN** the user navigates to the home page (index)
- **THEN** an AI content disclaimer is visible.
- **AND** the disclaimer text states that some works may contain AI-generated content or involve AI assistance in writing and editing.
