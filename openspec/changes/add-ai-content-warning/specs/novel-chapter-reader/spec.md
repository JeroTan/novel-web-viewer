## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AI Content Transparency Disclaimer on Chapter Reader
The chapter reader page SHALL display an AI content transparency disclaimer at the bottom of the chapter content, specifically after the chapter prose and before or after the bottom chapter navigation.

#### Scenario: User reaches the end of a chapter
- **WHEN** the user views a chapter and scrolls to the bottom
- **THEN** an AI content disclaimer is visible.
- **AND** the disclaimer text states that some works may contain AI-generated content or involve AI assistance in writing and editing.
