## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AI Content Transparency Disclaimer on List Page
The novel list page SHALL display an AI content transparency disclaimer at the bottom of the page, below the novel grid or empty state.

#### Scenario: User views the novel list page
- **WHEN** the user navigates to the novel list page
- **THEN** an AI content disclaimer is visible at the bottom of the page.
- **AND** the disclaimer text states that some works may contain AI-generated content or involve AI assistance in writing and editing.
